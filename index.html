You’re moving. You’re changing. But you’re not awake.

Not dead. Just drifting.

Not growing. Just shedding what hurts.

Each layer left behind leaves you smaller, not stronger.

You're afraid to stop.

Afraid of silence.

Afraid of becoming nothing.

So you keep crawling.

This is how we suffer.

This is how we inherit pain.

This is how we Crawl Asleep.
